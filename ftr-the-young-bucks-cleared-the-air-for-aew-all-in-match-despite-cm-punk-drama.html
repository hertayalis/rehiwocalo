FTR &amp; The Young Bucks Cleared The Air For AEW All In Match Despite CM Punk Drama

 Posted on August 21, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  306 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Dax Harwood and Cash Wheeler have earned a reputation as one of the most accomplished tag teams of the past decade in professional wrestling. Being part of AEW, they are aware of all the drama that went down in the past year. Due to all the drama surrounding CM Punk and Young Bucks, many believed a rematch between FTR and the Bucks would never happen. Of course, the match is now taking place at All In and it appears they cleared the air despite CM Punk’s drama.

CM Punk drew the ire of fans and pro wrestlers alike thanks to his rant after AEW All Out last year and the subsequent backstage brawl with The Elite. This ended up ruining AEW’s reputation in a big way, but the company has recovered.

As previously reported, Tony Khan stated that CM Punk is positive influence in the company. It is also a known fact that Punk and FTR are very close in real life and they have supported Punk throughout the drama.

It was officially confirmed that FTR and The Young Bucks will square off against each other at AEW All In, something that fans found surprising. According to the Wrestling Observer Newsletter, Dave Meltzer reported that FTR and The Young Bucks cleared the air in order to make the match a reality.

FTR, The Young Bucks, Adam Page and Kenny Omega cleared the air some time ago. When Tony Khan suggested Young Bucks vs. FTR for Wembley Stadium, it was a very quick yes from both sides.

FTR will be defending the AEW World Tag Team Championships against The Young Bucks on August 27th at All In. We’ll have to wait and see how the match will turn out.
