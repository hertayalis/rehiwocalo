The 15 Best Beauty Launches of March

 Posted on August 27, 2024  |  4 minutes  |  801 words  |  Aldo Pusey

As March comes to an end, we're looking back at all the beauty launches we've seen this month. And there were some serious good ones.

Matilda Djerf launched her own hair-care line; La Prairie revamped its beloved Skin Caviar Eye Lift Serum; and Laneige expanded its overnight mask family.

Shop these goodies and more as you browse the 15 best beauty launches of March below.

Djerf Avenue Beauty

Matilda Djerf — the fashion designer whose signature hair has inspired a movement of fluffy, layered cuts — has unveiled her very own hair-care line. Djerf Beauty is launching with two products: the On The Go Styling Gel ($22) and the Breeze Styling Mist ($27). The gel comes in both scented and unscented versions to add flexible hold, whether you're slicking back your strands or defining your curls. The mist adds volume and flexible hold while protecting strands from heat.

Estée Lauder x Sabyasachi Lipstick Collection

This limited-edition collaboration between fashion designer Sabyasachi and Estée Lauder consists of 10 stunning shades inspired by Sabyasachi's Indian roots. Think Tropical fruit and flora, hand-woven textiles, and glamorous gemstones. The shades come in ultra matte and satin matte finishes and are scented with warm, spicy cinnamon notes. 

La Prairie Skin Caviar Eye Lift

The Swiss brand's beloved eye serum just got a reformulation that includes the brand's Caviar Infinite and Caviar Micro-Nutrients. These resculpt the structure of the skin around the eyes by strengthening the skin's vertical pillars, leading to firmer, smoother, and lifted skin.

The Four Ways to Wellbeing

Nicola Elliot, former journalist and founder of beauty-wellness brand Neom Organics just published her first book. Elliot combined her knowledge garnered from 18 years in the wellness industry with her past in publishing (she spent 10 years working at U.K.-based magazines like Glamour, Marie Claire, and InStyle) to write a fabulously well-reported, science-based book. It thoughtfully dives into how to approach one's well-being through four pillars — getting better sleep, decreasing stress, increasing energy, and boosting your mood.

Laneige Bouncy + Firm Radiance Boosting Sleeping Mask

Expanding its sleeping mask family, Laneige has introduced its Bouncy & Firm formula. This buttery mask is moisturizing (thanks to a blend of glycerin and squalane) and firms your skin while you sleep with a peony-collagen complex, peptides, and green tea.

Keys Soulcare Firm Belief Smoothing Peptide Cream

This new cream is the sixth moisturizer in the Keys Soulcare lineup. It's packed with firming peptides and adaptogenic herbs, along with moisturizing snow mushroom to smooth the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.

Hello Sunday The Invisible One SPF 50 Hydrating Sunscreen + Primer

Hello Sunday, a sun-care brand that launched in the U.K. in 2021, just debuted stateside. Five of the brand's hero items are now available to shop at Sephora, and this moisturizer-primer duo is my favorite. It has a gorgeous texture that goes on smooth and hydrating before quickly drying down to a satin finish, providing the perfect base for makeup application.

Saie Slip Tint Radiant All-Over Concealer

For no-makeup makeup days, this new concealer from Saie is a godsend. It's a lightweight and creamy formula that can be used all over the face for a medium-coverage finish — meaning it can truly conceal any imperfections but with a natural-looking finish.

Remedy Skin

If you're on the beauty side of TikTok, you know Muneeb Shah, MD. He's a board-certified dermatologist in New York City who goes by DermDoctor on TikTok, where he's amassed over 18 million followers. The dermfluencer just launched his own skin-care line called Remedy which boasts three products for dark spots ($38), pore size ($32), and dry lips ($16), respectively.

Glossier The Complete Body Hero Collection

Glossier expanded its body-care line to include the Body Hero Daily Oil Wash ($28) and the Body Hero Daily Perfecting Lotion ($28), which can be bought separately or as part of this bundle. The oil wash is a soap-free cleanser designed to moisturize and cleanse, while the lotion provides lightweight, quick-drying moisture.

CoverGirl Clean Fresh Brow Nano

Fill your brows in with teeny-tiny hair-like strokes using this precise pencil from CoverGirl. It has a waterproof, smudge-proof formula that comes in six shades.

Kerasilk Strengthening Bond Builder

Kerasilk is one of the few brands I trust my strands with — so the launch of this bond-builder is especially exciting. It's an intensive treatment that blends the brand's Biomimetic Silk and Bondamide to repair brittle, dull, rough, and damaged strands by creating new bonds.

Peace Out Acne Gel Cleanser

To round out its acne-care offerings, Peace Out now has an acne cleanser. It blends 2% salicylic acid with maltobionic acid and ceramides to effectively exfoliate while strengthening the skin barrier and enhancing moisture retention.
